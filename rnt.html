what i took away from ratan tata

11 Oct, 2024
  • leading with empathy and truly understanding the needs of others is essential for building lasting, meaningful connections.
  • if i ever start a company, i will always prioritize people above everything else.
  • i will invest in those who are genuinely solving problems for the underserved and impoverished.
  • earth is not solely for humans; we share it with countless other forms of life, and our actions should reflect this understanding.
  • i will remember the kind souls who supported me during my growth and strive to reflect that same compassion toward those who are struggling today.
  • true immortality lies in living on in the hearts of others, achieved through selfless service to society.
